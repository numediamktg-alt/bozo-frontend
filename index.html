Who's Driving This Thing?

I'm Charles Paul Jones. 75 years old. Serial entrepreneur. Inventor. Fine art photographer with 62 years behind the camera. I hold a U.S. patent with 345 citations. I scaled a company to $100M revenue. And somewhere between all of that, I wrote 542 observations about life, wisdom, and the cosmic comedy.

Between November 1988 and June 1989, sitting in my home in Santa Fe, New Mexico, I wrote those quotes. I didn't know why. They just needed to be written.

In September 2007, I brought a Leica and a 33-megapixel medium format digital back to the Summer of Love anniversary and the Sausalito Art Festival. I made 197 photographs of a world that was about to disappear. Three months later, the iPhone arrived and concert crowds were never the same.

In 2026, planetary harmonic analysis connected the quotes to the photographs. The math says a quote from January 1989 and a photograph from September 2007 share a resonance pattern that matches YOUR birth chart TODAY. I built the system. The public gets to test it.
